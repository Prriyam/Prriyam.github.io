Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering — SVCE

My Journey at SVCE

I pursued my undergraduate degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering (affiliated with Anna University). My interest in engineering came from growing up around my father's aerospace manufacturing work, which shaped my curiosity for technology and problem-solving.

What I Studied

During my time at SVCE, I explored areas like digital electronics, microprocessors, communication systems, and signal processing. These subjects built the analytical foundation that later helped me transition into data and AI.

How I Moved Toward Data & AI

Alongside my coursework, I began exploring data science and programming through online platforms. I worked on small projects combining ECE concepts with data techniques — from signal processing improvements to embedded systems with intelligent behavior.

What I Took Away

This phase strengthened my technical fundamentals, curiosity, and ability to learn independently. It also helped me understand how engineering, data, and AI come together to solve real-world problems.
