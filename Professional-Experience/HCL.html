Building Intelligent Systems as a Technical Lead in Generative AI at HCLTech

Joining HCLTech America as a Technical Lead in Generative AI marked an exciting new chapter — one where I could bring together everything I had built over the years in data engineering, NLP, and AI to work on problems that genuinely push the boundary of what intelligent systems can do. From day one, I was immersed in designing and delivering agentic AI solutions for real enterprise challenges.

Accomplishments:

  • Built an autonomous ITOps support agent using the OpenAI SDK that reads ServiceNow incidents, performs knowledge-base-driven root-cause analysis, resolves issues end-to-end, and automatically updates tickets — significantly reducing manual intervention in IT operations.
  • Orchestrated a network observability agent that ingests PCAP data and leverages Neo4j Graph RAG to generate Cypher queries, enabling proactive detection of packet loss, congestion, and multi-device issues for NOC teams.
  • Delivered a Learning as a Service (LaaS) platform that unifies RAG-based Q&A retrieval, tutor-style interactive learning, and issue-resolution workflows into a single cohesive agent experience.
  • Designed an intelligent use-case evaluator that applies prompt engineering, evaluation metrics, and inter-logic reasoning to assess the feasibility of Agentic AI solutions — helping stakeholders make faster and more informed build decisions.
  • Engineered a real-time Metahuman communication system using LangChain agents, message queuing, and asynchronous streaming, achieving an 80% improvement in processing speed to enable fluid, interactive conversations.

Key Takeaways:

Working at HCLTech has reinforced my belief that Generative AI is most powerful when it is purposeful — grounded in real operational pain points and designed with reliability in mind. Every project here has challenged me to think beyond the model and consider the full system: the data pipelines feeding it, the workflows surrounding it, and the people ultimately depending on it.
