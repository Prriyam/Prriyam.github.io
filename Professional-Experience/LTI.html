My Journey from Graduate Engineering Trainee to Senior Data Engineer at LTI

Upon joining Larsen and Toubro Infotech (LTI), I embarked on a journey aligned with my passion for Data Science and Analytics. Recognizing the potential in this field, I dedicated myself to securing the position of Graduate Engineering Trainee during my initial campus placement interview. This role provided me with the opportunity to delve deep into the realm of Data Science and Analytics, establishing a robust foundation for my career.

Accomplishments:

  • Received extensive training in Business Intelligence & Data Warehouse (BIDW) over 3–4 months, enabling a comprehensive understanding of Data Engineering.
  • Functioned as an ETL developer for Citi Bank within the Banking & Financial Services domain, interacting directly with onshore clients to gather requirements and deliver solutions.
  • Specialized in building ETL pipelines using Ab Initio, enhancing existing models, and implementing Change Data Capture (CDC) and Slowly Changing Dimension (SCD) processes.
  • Resolved issues identified during Unit and Integration testing, ensuring seamless project execution.
  • Excelled in the niche area of Metadata Hub, overseeing a significant upgrade from version 4.x to the latest iteration within two months.
  • Demonstrated dedication by putting in extra hours to ensure Business as Usual (BAU) processes remained unaffected, earning client recognition and inclusion in the monthly newsletter.
  • Received a client accolade and a Pat on the Back award for my instrumental role in the Metadata Hub upgrade.
  • Earned client trust, leading to the responsibility of heading the Metadata Hub Team from India.

Extra-Curricular Activities:

  • Selected as one of seven members from LTI locations across India for the iWAW (Women Alchemy Workshop) program — a six-month initiative empowering women in leadership roles.
  • Actively participated in Corporate Social Responsibility initiatives including tree plantation drives, IT skills education for underprivileged children, and blood donation camps.
