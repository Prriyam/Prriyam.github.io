My Journey in the Field of AI at Kaaylabs

Upon joining Kaaylabs Inc., I embraced the opportunity to apply my skills in AI and Data Science to tackle complex problems, working on cutting-edge technologies in the AI domain. In this role, I was actively involved in designing and deploying AI models, working with advanced techniques such as LangChain and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and continually innovating to provide value to customers.

Accomplishments:

  • Designed and implemented AI models using LangChain and RAG techniques to develop a highly responsive and accurate QA chatbot, customized to meet the unique requirements of customers.
  • Migrated the AI model to Graph RAG techniques by building a comprehensive knowledge base using Neo4j, significantly enhancing data interrelation, improving model accuracy, and increasing contextual relevance.
  • Applied NLP techniques to extract state-related information from AI responses and developed an interactive choropleth map to visualize and analyze the extracted data, increasing user engagement and insight.
  • Implemented GitHub Actions to automate CI/CD workflows, streamlining the build, test, and deployment processes and reducing manual intervention.
  • Managed all phases of the SDLC — from gathering requirements and designing solutions to deploying and maintaining systems — ensuring timely and quality outcomes.

This role allowed me to continuously grow as a data-driven professional, honing my expertise in AI model design, data visualization, and automation. I remain dedicated to leveraging technical skills to innovate and drive impactful solutions.
