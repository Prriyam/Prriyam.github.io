My Journey from a Graduate Engineering Trainee to Senior Data Engineer at LTI

Upon joining Larsen and Toubro Infotech (LTI), I embarked on a journey aligned with my passion for Data Science and Analytics. Recognizing the potential in this field, I dedicated myself to securing the position of Graduate Engineering Trainee during my initial campus placement interview. This role provided me with the opportunity to delve deep into the realm of Data Science and Analytics, establishing a robust foundation for my career.

Accomplishments:

  • Received extensive training in Business Intelligence & Data Warehouse (BIDW) over a span of 3-4 months, enabling me to gain a comprehensive understanding of Data Engineering.
  • Functioned as an ETL developer for Citi Bank within the Banking & Financial Services (BFS) domain. This role involved direct interaction with clients from onshore countries, facilitating the gathering of business requirements and timely delivery of solutions.
  • Specialized in building ETL pipelines using Ab Initio, enhancing existing models, and implementing critical processes such as Change Data Capture (CDC) and Slowly Changing Dimension (SCD).
  • Resolved issues identified during Unit and Integration testing, ensuring seamless progress in project execution.
  • Excelled in the niche area of Metadata Hub, successfully overseeing a significant upgrade from version 4.x to the latest iteration within a remarkable timeframe of two months.
  • Demonstrated unwavering dedication, putting in extra hours, including weekends, to ensure that Business as Usual (BAU) processes remained unaffected, leading to client recognition and inclusion in the monthly newsletter's achievements section.
  • Received commendations for outstanding performance, including a client accolade and a Pat on the Back award from my Team Leader, for my instrumental role in the Metadata Hub upgrade.
  • Earned the trust of the client, leading to the responsibility of heading the Metadata Hub Team from India.
  • Gained invaluable experience in maintaining composure and control under pressure, effectively managing unforeseen issues, and reinforcing the belief that diligence and perseverance yield significant rewards.

Extra-Curricular Activities:

  • Selected as one of the seven distinguished members from LTI locations across India for the iWAW (Women Alchemy Workshop) program, a six-month dynamic intervention aimed at empowering women in leadership roles.
  • Actively participated in various Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives, including tree plantation drives, providing essential IT skills education to underprivileged and differently-abled children, and contributing to blood donation camps held at our office.
